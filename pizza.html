Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

For the dough

  • Pizza grade flour
  • Water
  • Eggs

For the sauce

  • Tomato pasata
  • Dried basil

Toppings

  • Mozarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  1. Mix the water and the flour with an egg to make the dough.
  2. Knead the dough well and stretch it.
  3. Roll it into a dough ball and leave it in an olive oil coated bowl. Cover and leave to rest and rise.
  4. Roll the dough out.
  5. Spread the sauce on top.
  6. Put cheese on top. Place the pepperoni on top.
  7. Put into the over and bake.
  8. Enjoy!
